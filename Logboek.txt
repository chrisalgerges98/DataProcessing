1. Are there any food prices that are show negative/positive correlation, and is this correlation present throughout the years, 
   or perhaps only in certain period? Can you perhaps detect possible ingredients of a certain other food product?

3. Can we see any correlations between other types of data, like: currency fluctuations, weather patterns, and/or refugee movements. 
   Can we perhaps visualize refugee movements from the food price data? Do certain weather conditions influence market prices?
